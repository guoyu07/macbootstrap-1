MacBootstrap

In this hastily-produced screencast series I show you how I setup my Mac OS X development environment from scratch with some very useful OS X power user tips along the way.

Episode 1

In this first episode, I literally just finished setting up a new computer. This will probably only be useful to people who are entirely now to the Mac ecosystem but hey, you might also learn something useful.
